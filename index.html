Our Story

Welcome to Pizza Station

At Pizza Station Navli, we believe that great food is the best way to bring the community together. Located in the heart of our vibrant town, we've grown into a local favorite by sticking to a simple philosophy: using the freshest ingredients to craft authentic, stone-baked pizzas that make you feel at home.

Every pizza we serve is a labor of love—from our 48-hour fermented sourdough crust to our locally sourced farm-fresh vegetables. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a gathering spot where friends and families share slices, stories, and smiles.
